Introduction to the Healthy Project App

The Healthy Project app is designed to assist busy individuals in managing their meals healthily and efficiently. This app offers a practical solution to meal planning challenges faced by people with hectic schedules.

Project Duration

July to August (Bootcamp Project)

Team Members

Jack He: Researchers, UI/UX Designer

Brian Chan: Researchers, UI/UX Designer

Aiden Ruiz: Researchers, UI/UX Designer

Problem Statement

Addressing the pressing need for convenient meal planning among busy individuals. The app aims to resolve the common issue of balancing nutritious meals with tight schedules.

The Solution

The Healthy Project app provides a comprehensive meal planner, giving users control over their meal choices. Whether dining out, ordering in, or batch cooking, the app offers adaptable solutions for every lifestyle.

Our App Goal

Purpose of Healthy food app is to assist people create a good balanced meal, save their time and give out all info that will be needed. App provides a wide range of healthy recipes, nutrition information, and meal tracking features to encourage user/busy people to make healthier food choices and maintain a good balanced diet. Also we have shopping list for users to buy ingredients at a good price.

Key Features

1. Flexible meal decision-making based on user preferences.

2. Advanced filters for healthy meal options.

3. Comprehensive nutritional information for informed choices.

Understanding Our Audience

Interview Plan

Our target audience for this app are people ages 25-30 years old. As such, we will be interviewing people in these age ranges who have busy lives and could potentially benefit from a meal planning app.
