Virtual Room Decor dApp

Project Context

NFT Decor is a groundbreaking decentralized application (dApp) designed to transform the way NFT collectors display and interact with their digital art. This project aims to create an engaging, social, and customizable space for showcasing NFT collections.

Project Duration and Tools

Date: November 2023

Duration: 3.5 weeks

Tools: Figma, Spline, Adobe Photoshop, Google Suite

Project Overview

Focusing on building a dApp, NFT Decor serves as a unique social network where users can personalize virtual rooms to exhibit their NFT collections. It bridges art, technology, and social interaction in the NFT space.

User's Challenge

Problem Statement

NFT collectors face challenges in displaying their collections in a unified, interactive space while building social connections within the NFT community.

Solution Concept

NFT Decor is conceptualized to offer a dynamic and adaptable platform for users to display their NFTs in creative virtual environments, thereby enhancing social engagement and network building.

Understanding the Market and Users

Competitor Analysis

Our analysis of competitors like Christie's and emerging NFT display apps has sharpened our focus for the NFT Decor project. We found that while some platforms offer excellent display options, they may lack a strong community aspect, which is crucial for NFT enthusiasts. Others might be user-friendly but fall short in integrating social features. With NFT Decor, we aim to fill these gaps by creating a welcoming environment that not only showcases NFTs impressively but also fosters genuine connections among users. By combining the best features from our competitors and addressing their shortcomings, we're crafting an NFT display experience that is both visually stunning and community-driven.

Gathering In-Depth User Insights

Interview

We planned and conducted 5 interviews with potential users who owns NFTs. In order to create a social network where users can decorate their room with their personal NFT collection, we analyzed the user pain points and insights.

Interview Transcript

In one-on-one interviews, it became evident that users' primary concern revolved around their desire to showcase their NFTs but lacked a more suitable platform for doing so.

Survey Data

During our project, we conducted a survey that garnered 12 responses, offering valuable insights into the preferences and behaviors of NFT collectors.

Socialization Trends Among Collectors

A significant 58% of survey participants indicated they do not socialize with other NFT collectors either online or in real life. Additionally, 25% chose not to answer this question, suggesting a potential gap in social engagement within the NFT community.

Preferences for NFT Display

When asked about their preferred method of showcasing NFTs in a virtual space, the responses were evenly split. Both displaying NFTs on walls like a gallery and incorporating them as part of a virtual environment received 25% preference, indicating a desire for diverse and creative display options.

Desired Features in NFT Art Rooms

A notable finding from the survey was that a majority (10 out of 12 respondents) expressed a strong preference for "Interactive displays" in customizable NFT art rooms. This highlights the user's desire for engaging and dynamic features that enhance their experience of interacting with NFTs.

Insight Statement

We believe providing an app that promotes socialization between users through a unique and interactive NFT display method will make a more welcoming and enjoyable NFT collecting community.

Overview of Key Features

The Feature Prioritization Matrix for NFT Decor was developed to identify and focus on the most crucial aspects of the app. This matrix helped streamline our development process by highlighting the features most valued by our target users.

Virtual Room for Decorating with NFTs

This feature allows users to create and customize virtual rooms where they can artistically display their NFT collections. It's a core aspect of the app, providing a personalized space for users to express their style and preferences.

Features for Rooms and Collections

A significant feature of NFT Decor is the ability for users to browse through others' virtual rooms and collections. This not only fosters a sense of community but also allows users to see the value of items in others' collections, promoting transparency and a deeper understanding of the NFT market.

Social Media Integration & Display of NFT Values

The integration of social media functionalities is a critical feature. It enables users to share their virtual NFT rooms and collections on various social platforms, enhancing visibility and social engagement. Additionally, the app displays the value of NFTs within these virtual rooms, providing useful insights into the worth of the collections and individual pieces.

Design Process and Development

Paper Wireframe

In the first phase of our NFT Decor project, we created paper wireframes to establish the basic structure of our app. These wireframes outlined the primary functions and the user's path through the app, providing a clear initial concept for our design.

Sketches and Concept

Our sketches begin with a simple approach, illustrating the initial user interaction with our app. The focus at this stage is on ensuring a clear and engaging start to the user experience.

User Sign Up and Login

We mapped out the user sign-up and login process. This includes options for new users to sign up and existing users to log into their wallets, ensuring a seamless entry point into the app.

Room Decoration Setup

Post-login, users are guided to a setup phase where they can start decorating a virtual room. This stage is crucial as it's where users begin personalizing their space, a key feature of our app.

Navigating to Home Page with Features Wireframes

Once the initial setup is complete, users are directed to the home page. Here, they can access various features of the app in a user-friendly and intuitive interface.

Beyond the initial setup, our wireframes include designs for:

Top Viewed Rooms: A feature that allows users to explore and get inspired by the most popular virtual rooms in the community.

Inventory Access: A straightforward way for users to manage and view their NFT collections.

Decorate Mode: An interactive and creative space where users can customize their virtual rooms with their NFTs.

Social Sharing: Functionality that enables users to share their decorated rooms on social media platforms, fostering community engagement and showcasing their unique collections.

Key Learning From User Test

1. Navigation bar icons are unclear because there’s no text indicating the function of the CTA.

2. Need something to indicate that user can scroll over to see more on the Decoration Mode page.

3. User believes she should be aware of that the next step of logging in is upload collection.

4. User would like to see a notification showing up on the navigation bar so that she knows that there is a friend request.

UI Style Sheet

For the NFT Decor app, our mood board was a creative amalgamation of various UI styles, combining elements of futurism with a cyberpunk aesthetic. The chosen style direction, incorporating "Visual," "Balanced," and "Cyberpunk" themes, resulted in a user interface that is both visually captivating and functionally elegant. This approach integrated futuristic interfaces, minimalistic design elements, and vibrant neon accents, all contributing to a uniquely engaging user experience.

Our UI Style Adjectives - "Cyberpunk," "Visual," "Balanced," "Futuristic," "Neon Glow," and "Dark Mode" - collectively describe the essence of our design ethos. Each adjective played a pivotal role in guiding the aesthetic decisions, from layout compositions to color schemes and typography.

Speaking of typography, we chose 'Russo One' and 'Darker Grotesque' fonts for their bold and modern appeal, complementing our app's cyberpunk and futuristic theme. These fonts add a contemporary edge to the app's overall design, ensuring readability and style.

In our quest for the perfect logo, we explored various designs and finally settled on one that encapsulates the essence of NFT style. This logo not only aligns with our app’s theme but also resonates with the NFT community.

Our color palette for the NFT Decor app is chosen to not only enhance the app's futuristic and cyberpunk theme but also to ensure optimal accessibility. The selection includes a deep black, vibrant cyan, bright neon green, and soft lavender, balanced with classic white. These colors work together to create a visually appealing experience while passing the AAA accessibility test, ensuring a comfortable and inclusive user experience for everyone.

Promotional Website for NFT Decor

Introduction Of Our Promotional Webpage

Our primary objective is to captivate and inform visitors, guiding them towards downloading and engaging with our app. The webpage is crafted to highlight the unique features and benefits of our app, providing a glimpse into the value it offers.

For this promotional webpage, we've adhered to our established UI style, utilizing the same color palette to ensure brand consistency. Also maintained uniformity in our typography, using the Russo One and Darker Grotesque fonts, with chosen font sizes to enhance readability and visual appeal.

Our website features a user-friendly navigation bar that highlights our top features, such as 'Top Rooms' and 'Popular NFTs', guiding users to current trends and popular choices.

The main page spotlights our app through an eye-catching promotional post, designed to immediately grab the user's attention.

Below the main promotion, we showcase the app's features, giving new users insight into what our app offers and how it stands out.

We've created the content to not only inform but also excite potential users about our app. The page includes engaging calls to action, like 'Sounds great? Download it now!', paired with a prominent download button.

The download page offers a choice between app stores, catering to diverse user preferences.

For users who wish to explore more before downloading, we've added convenient navigation options. A 'back to top' button in the bottom right corner allows users to quickly return to the top of the page, where they can choose to explore 'Top Rooms' or 'Popular NFTs' before deciding to download.
